Essential Services Offered by KNEC

The Kenya National Examination Council is an independent organization tasked by the government with overseeing the implementation of top-notch national examinations.
In addition, the council, in collaboration with other organizations like the TSC, is responsible for overseeing the administration, supervision, marking, standardization and publication of these exams.
The mandate of KNEC is outlined in Section 10 of KNEC Act No. 29 of 2012 as to:

‚úìUndertake research on educational assessment
